about

I'm a Creative Director with over 8 years of experience in the commercial agency space. A native to Los Angeles, I've grown up in the arts and entertainment community for most of my life. My Work has taken me to the far reaches of the globe working on location from studios in Los Angeles to the far reaches of the Himalayan Mountains. I've commanded crews as large as 40 men and women as well as documentary humble crews as large as 6. In my career, I've been blessed to have worked with brands such as Google, Apple, Vimeo, Toyota, IMAX, Samsung, Slack, VICE, NOS, The UPS Store, Major League Soccer, Slack, Discover LA, Spindrift, Onnit, and many more. When I'm not working I spend time with my wife and daughter in Culver City or spearfishing and diving off the coast of Los Angeles.

I am a complete social animal. Find my work on Vimeo & GITHUB. Experience my photography skills on Instagram & My Website.
